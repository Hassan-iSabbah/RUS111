The process of organisation

Layer One

The fastest way to become organised is simply to get everything that you do have and then start Getting Boxes/Containers and storing them all into categories.

Layer Two

Organising the Future is incredibly important as it determines if you bring an umbrella on the correct day, and if you can find a time slot simply in your mind before confirming if it is on paper. Meditation is key, and breathing will oil the lock.

Layer Three

Steve Jobs used to use a system called Signal, Noise where Signal is basically everything that must be done within the next 48 hours, the three biggest goals while noise is all the problems currently experienced. Priorities constantly shift as we go about life, and so can our intention... so lets focus on editing their priorities too

Three layers to organisation, because everyone always wants to be around organised people.
